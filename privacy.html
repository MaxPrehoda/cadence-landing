Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

At Cadence, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This policy explains how we handle your data — spoiler: we barely collect any, and what we do collect stays on your device.

TL;DR

  • All voice processing happens locally on your Mac. Your audio never leaves your device.
  • No cloud services. No servers. No external APIs for transcription.
  • Minimal analytics. We use Vercel Web Analytics (privacy-friendly, no cookies) to understand basic usage patterns.
  • No account required. No tracking. No user profiles.

What We Collect

Information You Provide

None. Cadence doesn't require an account, login, or any personal information to function.

Audio & Voice Data

All voice transcription is performed locally on your device using Whisper.cpp. Your voice recordings:

  • Never leave your Mac
  • Are not stored permanently (processed in memory only)
  • Are never uploaded to any server or cloud service
  • Are immediately discarded after transcription

Website Analytics

Our website uses Vercel Web Analytics, which:

  • Collects anonymous page views and basic traffic data
  • Does NOT use cookies or track you across sites
  • Does NOT collect personally identifiable information
  • Complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

Learn more: Vercel Analytics Privacy Policy

macOS Permissions

Cadence requires the following macOS permissions to function:

  • Microphone Access — To capture voice commands (processed locally)
  • Accessibility Access — To automate system tasks and control applications
  • Screen Recording — To detect active windows and context for automation

You can revoke these permissions anytime via System Settings → Privacy & Security.

How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal data, there's not much to "use." Here's what happens:

  • Voice commands are processed locally and used only to execute the actions you request
  • Website analytics help us understand which features are most popular and improve the product

Third-Party Services

Cadence does NOT integrate with any third-party services for core functionality. The only external service is:

  • Vercel Web Analytics (website only, privacy-friendly)

We do NOT use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook Pixel
  • Ad networks
  • Cloud transcription services (OpenAI, Google Speech, etc.)

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: Cadence stores minimal application preferences locally on your Mac using standard macOS storage APIs. This includes settings like keyboard shortcuts and automation preferences.

No Cloud Sync: We don't sync your data to the cloud. Your settings stay on your device.

Security: All data processing happens within macOS's sandboxed environment with the permissions you grant.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (but we don't collect personal data to access)
  • Delete your data (uninstall the app — no server-side data exists)
  • Opt-out of analytics (use browser extensions or disable JavaScript on our website)

Children's Privacy

Cadence does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from anyone, this is a non-issue.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Cadence after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Bottom line: We built Cadence to respect your privacy. Your voice stays on your device. No tracking. No surveillance. Just local, powerful voice control.
